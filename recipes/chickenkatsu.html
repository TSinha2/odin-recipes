Chicken Katsu

Chicken katsu is Japanese-style fried chicken. This is can also be used to make tonkatsu by using pork cutlets instead of chicken. Serve with white rice and tonkatsu sauce.

Steps:

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
